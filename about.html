ABOUT MEDANTA HOSPITAL.

Medanta is a multi-specialty healthcare provider in India, known for its tertiary care services and advanced medical facilities. Founded by Dr. Naresh Trehan, a renowned cardiovascular surgeon, Medanta aims to deliver high-quality, patient-centric, and affordable healthcare. It's recognized for its team-based, doctor-led approach and comprehensive range of specialties. [1, 2] Here's a more detailed look at Medanta: Key Features: • Multi-specialty: Medanta offers a wide range of medical specialties, including cardiac care, cancer care, neurosciences, gastrosciences, orthopaedics, and renal care. [2] • Tertiary Care: Medanta focuses on providing advanced and specialized medical treatment, often referred to as tertiary care. [2, 3] • Advanced Technology: Medanta utilizes cutting-edge technology and infrastructure to deliver the best possible care, according to Medanta. [2] • Focus on Patient Care: The hospital emphasizes a patient-centric approach, aiming to provide compassionate and holistic care. [2, 4, 5, 6] • Doctor-Led Model: Medanta operates with a team-based, doctor-led model, ensuring expertise and collaboration in patient management. [2] • Ranked among the best: Medanta has been consistently ranked as one of the best private hospitals in India, says Medanta. [1, 2] • Locations: Medanta has several hospitals and clinics across India, including locations in Gurugram, Lucknow, Patna, Indore, Ranchi, and Delhi. [2, 3] • Affordable Healthcare: Medanta strives to make advanced medical services accessible and affordable to a wider population, states Medanta. [1]
